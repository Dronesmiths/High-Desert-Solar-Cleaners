Bird Proofing: Protecting Your Solar Investment

Published by High Desert Solar Cleaners

Pigeons love solar panels. The gap between your roof and the panels provides shade, shelter, and protection from predators—making it the perfect nesting ground. Unfortunately, this creates havoc for your home.

Why Birds Love Solar Arrays

In the exposed High Desert environment, shade is a premium commodity. Your solar array acts as a giant umbrella, inviting birds to roost underneath. Once one family moves in, they establish territory, leading to a rapidly growing infestation that is noisy and messy.

The Hazard: Acidic Droppings & Fire Risk

Bird droppings are highly acidic and can corrode wiring and backsheets over time. Worse, the accumulation of dry nesting material—twigs, leaves, and feathers—underneath hot electrical components creates a genuine fire hazard. Keeping this area clear is critical for safety.

Our Humane Mesh Solution

We install a specialized, PVC-coated steel mesh around the perimeter of your array. This creates a physical barrier that prevents birds from entering while still allowing proper airflow. It's a permanent, humane solution that protects your system without harming the wildlife.

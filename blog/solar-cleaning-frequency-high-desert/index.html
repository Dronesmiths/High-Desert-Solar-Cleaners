Cleaning Frequency Guide for the AV

Published by High Desert Solar Cleaners

Living in the Antelope Valley involves contending with dust, wind, and pollen. These factors mean your solar panels need more frequent attention than in coastal areas.

The "6-Month Rule"

For most residential homes in Palmdale and Lancaster, we recommend a cleaning service every 6 months. This typically aligns with:
1. Post-Winter: Cleaning off winter rain spots and grime.
2. Post-Summer: Removing the thick layer of summer dust and pollen.

When to Increase Frequency

You might need quarterly cleaning if:

  • You live near a dirt field or construction site.
  • You have large trees shedding pollen nearby.
  • You notice massive efficiency drops on your monitoring app.

Why Rain Isn't Enough

Rain in the High Desert is often just a "mud bath." It grabs the dust from the air and deposits it on your panels. Once it evaporates, it leaves a hard mineral residue that blocks light. Only a DI water scrub can truly clean it.

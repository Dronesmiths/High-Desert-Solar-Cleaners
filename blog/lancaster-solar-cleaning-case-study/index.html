Case Study: Restoring Efficiency in West Lancaster

Published by High Desert Solar Cleaners

Meet Sarah T. from West Lancaster. After installing her system in 2023, she noticed her True-Up bill was slowly creeping up, despite having the same energy usage. The culprit? Two years of accumulated dust.

The Problem: 2 Years of Neglect

Sarah's 24-panel array was covered in a thick layer of fine desert silt and pollen. While it only looked "a little dusty" from the ground, on closer inspection, the film was blocking a significant amount of UV radiation. Her monitoring app showed a steady 1.5% decline in production month-over-month.

The Solution: De-ionized Wash

Our team arrived at 8:00 AM. Using our specialized water-fed pole system and triple-filtered De-ionized water, we gently scrubbed and rinsed every panel. The entire process took less than 2 hours and used zero harsh chemicals—just pure water that dries completely spot-free.

The Result: 28% Output Increase

The difference was immediate. Sarah checked her monitoring app at noon that same day and saw a 28% spike in production compared to the previous clear day. By restoring her system to peak performance, she offset the cost of the cleaning in just a few months of utility savings.

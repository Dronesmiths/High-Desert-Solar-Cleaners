The Pigeon Problem

Solar panels provide perfect shelter for birds. Unfortunately, their nesting materials can cause heat buildup and fire hazards, and their acidic droppings can permanently damage your roof and panels.

We install humane, high-quality galvanized steel mesh (Critter Guard) around the perimeter of your array. It looks clean, allows airflow, but keeps birds out permanently.

Benefits

  • Protect Your Roof: Stop corrosive droppings from eating into your underlayment.
  • Reduce Fire Risk: Remove dry nesting material from hot electrical components.
  • 5-Year Guarantee: Our installations are built to last.

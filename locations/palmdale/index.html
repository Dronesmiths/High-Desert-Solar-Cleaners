Why Palmdale Homes Need Professional Cleaning

Palmdale's unique position in the High Desert subjects solar panels to high winds, dust storms, and pollen from local agriculture. This debris accumulation creates a "soiling loss" that can reduce your system's efficiency by 25-30% if left unchecked.

We provide specialized cleaning using De-ionized (DI) water, which is free of mineral content. Unlike hose water, which leaves calcium deposits (hard water spots) that can permanently damage your glass, our DI water dries spot-free, ensuring maximum light absorption.
